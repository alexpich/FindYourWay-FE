Help

What is FindYourWay?

FindYourWay is an application that will asks users a series of questions. Based on the users' locations and responses, FindYourWay will give recommendations for the users to choose from.

How do I get started?

You must first login or register to start using our service. Once logged in, you will be prompted to enter your location and get started answering the series of questions.

How do I earn points?

You can earn points by simply using our service. Logging in, making a search, or favoriting a location will add points to your account!

What are points?

Points are our way of rewarding users for using our service. Once you collect enough points, you can redeem them for badges and coupons!

How do I save a location to my favorites?

When you view a favorite, there is a button to save the location to your favorites page which is available after you log in.
